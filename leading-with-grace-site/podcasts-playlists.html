Podcasts & Playlists

Curated audio resources to fuel leadership thinking on the go.

The Podcasts & Playlists section offers audio-based learning you can listen to while driving, walking, or between meetings. These episodes and playlists are chosen for their relevance to practical leadership— especially motivating teams, communicating with clarity, handling conflict, and leading through change. Short summaries and reflection prompts are provided to help transform listening into action.

Overview

Each recommended resource includes:

  • Episode summary and why it matters to everyday leadership
  • Suggested listening context (solo reflection, team discussion, 1:1 coaching)
  • Questions to consider while or after listening
  • (Optional) music playlists to support focus or recovery during heavy leadership seasons

Dare to Lead • Brené Brown

Topic: Courageous Leadership · Format: Podcast Series

Brené Brown explores vulnerability, leading with clarity, and navigating difficult conversations. The series offers practical insights from leaders across industries.

Listen for: How to pair accountability with compassion.

Reflect: “What would courageous leadership look like in my next hard conversation?”

Listen on Spotify

Craig Groeschel Leadership Podcast

Topic: Vision & Practical Leadership

Focused, actionable leadership insights covering trust, performance, values-aligned culture, and leading teams through uncertainty.

Try this: Choose one small leadership adjustment to implement for 30 days.

Coaching for Leaders • Dave Stachowiak

Topic: Coaching & Team Development

Practical episodes on effective 1:1s, growth conversations, and helping people perform at their highest level.

Topic: Motivation, Performance & Psychology at Work

Organizational psychologist Adam Grant dives into what makes work environments meaningful, collaborative, and sustainable—with a healthy balance of practicality and humor.

Leadership Music Playlists

Music can help leaders regulate energy between high-pressure tasks, presentations, or difficult meetings. Suggested playlists may include:

  • Focus & Clarity – Instrumental or soft electronic for deep work
  • High Energy – Upbeat tracks for pre-presentation confidence
  • Reflection & Reset – Ambient tracks for recovery after intensive dialogue

These can be curated and added over time as custom Spotify or Apple Music playlists.
