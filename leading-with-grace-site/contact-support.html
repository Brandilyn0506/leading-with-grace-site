Contact & Support

Questions, ideas, or tech hiccups—this is where to start.

The Contact & Support page is here to help you get what you need, whether that’s clarity on a concept, support with resources, or a way to share feedback. If you’re using Leading with Grace & Truth materials in your own leadership, classroom, or organization, this is your place to ask for guidance and stay connected.

Overview

You can use this page to:

  • Ask questions about using Grace & Truth tools, guides, or videos.
  • Request support for workshops, team sessions, or leadership development programs.
  • Share feedback on what’s working well and what you’d like to see more of.
  • Sign up for updates, new resources, and leadership reflections.

If your question doesn’t fit neatly into a category, that’s okay—reach out anyway. The goal is to support you as you lead with both grace and truth in real, everyday situations.

FAQ

Below are some common questions about using this site, the resources, and how to get support.

Who is this site for?

This site is for leaders and emerging leaders who want practical, values-centered tools. It’s designed to support people leading teams, projects, classrooms, or communities—anyone responsible for influencing others.

Can I use these resources with my own team?

Yes. You are encouraged to use the concepts, questions, and frameworks with your team or small group. If you’d like help tailoring content to your context or building a session around it, you can submit a request through the contact form.

What if I have a specific leadership situation I’d like help with?

You can describe your situation using the contact form below or through the Ask a Mentor space (if available on this site). Please remove names and identifying details and focus on the behavior, context, and outcome you’re hoping for.

Do you offer coaching or workshops?

Coaching and workshops may be available depending on scheduling and fit. Use the contact form to share what you’re looking for, and someone will follow up with options and next steps.

How do I report a technical issue with the site?

If a page, video, or resource isn’t working as expected, include the page name, what you were trying to do, and any error message you saw in the contact form. This helps troubleshoot quickly and get things working smoothly again.

Contact Form

Newsletter Sign-up

If you’d like occasional leadership reflections, prompts, and updates about new resources, you can join the Grace & Truth email list. The goal is simple: helpful, practical content—not inbox clutter.

You can unsubscribe at any time. In a live site, this form would connect to an email service provider.

What you can expect

  • Short reflections on leading with grace and truth.
  • Tools and prompts you can use with your team.
  • Occasional updates on new guides, videos, or sessions.
