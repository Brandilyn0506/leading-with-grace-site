Approaching Conflict with Grace & Courage

Conflict is unavoidable whenever people care about their work. The goal is not to avoid conflict, but to handle it in ways that protect relationships and surface truth. New leaders may feel pressure to “keep the peace” and accidentally allow resentment to build.

  • Pause before reacting; seek to understand the story behind each person’s position.
  • Use “both/and” language to name shared goals alongside differences.
  • Focus the conversation on issues and behaviors, not on personal character.
  • Agree on concrete next steps and how you will check back in.
