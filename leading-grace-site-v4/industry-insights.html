Industry Insights

Curated articles, research, and trends to support thoughtful, evidence-informed leadership.

The Industry Insights page highlights select leadership resources that are practical, research-backed, and aligned with the principles of Leading with Grace & Truth. Each insight includes a brief summary, why it matters, and a suggested way to apply it in real leadership moments.

Overview

Each resource is chosen with three goals in mind:

  • Clarity: Translate complex ideas into practical leadership insights.
  • Relevance: Focus on what leaders face in real conversations and decision-making.
  • Action: Provide a next step or reflection to apply the concept.
